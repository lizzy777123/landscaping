Our Story

Crafting Heritage Landscapes Since 2008

Founded in the heart of Manchester, Evergreen was born from a desire to redefine the relationship between our city's rugged industrial history and the tranquil possibilities of private green spaces.

Our commitment to craftsmanship is rooted in the traditions of North West stonemasons and horticulturalists. Every project we undertake is a tribute to the local heritage—from using reclaimed Yorkstone that echoes our city's streets to selecting native species that thrive in the unique Manchester microclimate.

"We don't just build gardens; we curate outdoor narratives that stand as long-term investments for Manchester's finest homes."

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical garden renovation take? expand_more
Small to medium renovations typically take 2-3 weeks. Larger, more complex projects involving structural work or multi-level terracing can take 4-8 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the quote stage.
Do you offer free on-site consultations? expand_more
Yes, we provide an initial site visit and no-obligation quote for all projects within Greater Manchester and Cheshire. This allows us to understand your vision and assess the site conditions.
Is your work guaranteed? expand_more
Absolutely. All of our structural landscaping work is guaranteed for 5 years, and our paving comes with a manufacturer-backed warranty on materials alongside our installation guarantee.
